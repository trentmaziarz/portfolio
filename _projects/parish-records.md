A collaborative platform for transcribing and structuring early modern parish records using OCR and crowdsourced verification.

Parish records are among the richest sources for early modern social history, but their handwritten formats make large-scale analysis difficult. This platform combines automated OCR with a crowdsourced review workflow to produce structured, searchable datasets from scanned parish registers.
